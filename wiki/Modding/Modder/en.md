# Modder

A **modder** is a user who helps other beatmap creators improve their [beatmaps](/wiki/Beatmap) through [modding](/wiki/Modding). Successful posts on a [beatmap discussion](/wiki/Beatmap_discussion) awards the modder [Kudosu!](/wiki/Modding/Kudosu) which can be seen on the user's profile page.
