[**Click here to start voting in the Spring 2024 Fanart Contest!**](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/contests/205)

Voting stage will be open for only **7 days** as of the creation of this post, so be sure to hop on the bandwagon quick before you're left behind with some shiny votes in need of spending!

Many thanks to all participants who chose to pour their time and sweat into submitting an entry — you're the lifeblood of this contest, and all art enjoyers in our community are eternally grateful!
