We have concluded our osu!idol contest for this year. Thank you to everyone that was able to participate in the [community voting](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/contests/83)! It's time for the results.

![](/wiki/shared/news/2019-11-19-osu-idol-2019-finals-community-voting/banner.jpg)

### Results

Congratulations to this year's osu!idol: **[iamALieee](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/10093980)**! Their amazing performance of Arisa Komiya's *White First Love* acquired 1,283 votes, earning them first place! They will be rewarded with the osu!idol 2019 Winner profile badge and 6 months of osu!supporter.

Coming up next we have **[aceredshirt13](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/13736238)** with their performance of Darren Korb's *Setting Sail, Coming Home*, taking second with 290 votes and receiving 3 months of osu!supporter!

In a close third, with 204 votes, is **[moof](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/8457202)** with their performance of niki's *Jitter Doll*! They will be taking home 2 months of osu!supporter. Another job well done!

You can listen to their performances here:

- [iamALieee - White First Love](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/83/entries/osu!idol%202019%20-%20iamALieee%20-%20White%20First%20Love.mp3)
- [aceredshirt13 - Setting Sail, Coming Home](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/83/entries/osu!idol%202019%20-%20aceredshirt13%20-%20Setting%20Sail,%20Coming%20Home.mp3)
- [moof - Jitter Doll](https://assets.ppy.sh/contests/83/entries/osu!idol%202019%20-%20moof%20-%20Jitter%20Doll.mp3)

As this year's contest comes to a close, we would like to thank the community for making this possible! Remember to check out our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/7hZgNeH) for chatting, karaoke and future announcements. See you next time!
