**ALLMYFRIENDS** packs our Featured Artist library with new tracks from over **10** talented artists across the rhythm game scene (including some names that we're sure you'll recognise).

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/374/header.jpeg)

These guys get straight to the point: **ALLMYFRIENDS** is simply a group of friends who love to make and release music together!

We picked up **8** songs from their debut album *PRISMA*, all ready for mapping from [**ALLMYFRIENDS**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/374) and highlighting these top-tier artists:
