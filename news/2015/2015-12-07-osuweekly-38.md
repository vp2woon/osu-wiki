The finals for the OWC is at last here! The moment you've all been waiting for; China will be competing with the U.S for the OWC!2015 Crown. Go to the [osu!live twitch channel](http://www.twitch.tv/osulive) if you want to watch the match, as it will be streamed live during December 12 to December 13 2015. The winner will be shown sometime after the event. Click [here](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/371786) for more details.

[Times almost up to buy those cute profile banners in support of the OWC2015!](https://osu.ppy.sh/store/products/38) To recap, If you so happen to own the banner of the winning country/team; the banner will last for a period of time after the end of the tournament along with a special design to accommodate. Get them while you can!

Our very own [peppy](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/peppy) will be streaming in the upcoming weeks. The stream will consist of mostly coding. If you're into such things then please visit his [twitch channel.](http://twitch.tv/ppy)

We have new blood in the Quality Assurance Team (QAT). [Blocko](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/Blocko) has joined the staff as part of the QAT, specializing in the osu!mania mode. Give him a nice greeting if you happen to see him.

[Notice to people who purchased osu!nono keyboards](https://blog.ppy.sh/post/134637068903/20151206) : Some of the keyboards have a faulty LED. To those who have bought one recently, and have seen that your right hand LED backlight is consistently dimmer than you're left; Contact the osu!store staff by replying to your confirmation email for a replacement. Apologies for the inconvenience caused.

NVIDIA Users Rejoice! [The newest release has fixed pretty much all the stuttering and lag on newer NVIDIA hardware plus many more other fixes.](https://osu.ppy.sh/p/changelog?v=b20151202.3) Click on the previous link to see all the changes!

That's a wrap. Leave any suggestions you may have on future news to **[news@ppy.sh](mailto:news@ppy.sh)** and we'll take a look. Have an enjoyable week!
