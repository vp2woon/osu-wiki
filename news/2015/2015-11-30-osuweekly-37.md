The osu! world cup is approaching the finals! With only 3 weeks left in the competition, only 1 more week till finals weeks. The winning team will soon be shown! [View which teams are still left in the competition plus more info in the official wiki page.](https://osu.ppy.sh/wiki/Osu!_World_Cup_2015)

[osu!store has introduced osu! USB sticks](https://blog.ppy.sh/post/133995831158/20151126), osu! small flash drives that allow you to store and transmit information. As of now it has 80MB/s write and 200MB/s Read consistent. Avaiable in limited supply in the osu!store in either December or January.

[osu! tablets are stocking back up; expect the next batch soon](https://osu.ppy.sh/store/products/1), at least before christmas, expect more merchadaise available in the osu!store in the near future.

An old pro player has returned to osu!, [Cookiezi](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/cookiezi). Known for making incredible plays in the past, Has come back to regain his place. Look at his profile for more info on him; including his twitch channel (the place he live streams!)

To all the ALiCE'S EMOTiON Fans out there; A beatmap pack has been made by Ukami which includes songs made by REDALiCE, t+pazolite and Ayumi Nomiya. [Download it in the beatmap packs and find other beatmap packs your interested in by clicking here.](https://osu.ppy.sh/p/packlist?t=a)

So Please Vote!!!!!!! Voting will  close November 30th 23:59:59 UTC. Try to get your votes in before that time.
