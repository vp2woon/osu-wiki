Fond of catching some fruit? Fancy yourself some manic keypressing? Voting for the March and April Monthly Beatmapping Contests is now open - featuring both osu!catch and osu!mania.

[![](https://assets.ppy.sh/media/mbc-march-april-header.jpg)](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/contests)

If you're after some new maps to play, check out the voting for the [March osu!catch monthly beatmapping contest](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/contests/44) and the [April osu!mania monthly beatmapping contest](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/contests/46).

There's over a dozen entries for each, and we need your help in determining the winner! Head on over to the contest listings using the links above, download the maps and make your decision on who's map is the best of the best.

Voting will remain open for 14 days from the date of this post, and we'll be announcing the winners shortly after the vote has concluded.

The next monthly beatmapping contest to be announced after the osu!taiko Aspire round concludes will be for the osu! gamemode, so if you're planning to compete in that, make sure you're well warmed up for it!
