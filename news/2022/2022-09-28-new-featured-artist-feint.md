Legendary drum and bass producer **Feint** has finally joined our Featured Artist collection!

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/306/header.jpg)

Drum and bass music on osu! wouldn't be as prolific as it is today without **Feint**. Classic beatmaps like [*Time Bomb*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/98842) by [vipto](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/838877) and [*Snake Eyes*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/130223) by [galvenize](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/381444) set the stage for this evergreen genre, leading to an endless library of **Feint** and drum and bass in general throughout osu!.

**Feint** has undoubtedly shaped the history of osu!, and as a Featured Artist, we're excited to see how his music shapes the future of osu! too.

**31** songs spanning from [*Monstercat*](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/255), *Liquicity* and even solo releases are now available for our mapping community. Take a peek at [**Feint**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/306) for the full list of songs and their beatmap templates!

As an osu! player, you're surely well-versed in **Feint**'s sound, but it wouldn't hurt to revisit any of the beatmaps below or witness a showcase of osu!(lazer)'s new default skin *argon* in this video:
