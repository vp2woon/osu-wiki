**Tokyo Machine** leaps from the [**Monstercat**](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/255) catalogue into our Featured Artists!

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/276/header.jpg)

One of EDM's most distinct creators, **Tokyo Machine** raises the bar with an exceptional combination of heavy bass and Japan-inspired 8-bit concepts. This artist is no stranger to the gaming scene, with official features in major titles like *Rocket League*, *Just Shapes & Beats*, and *Beat Saber*.

Start mapping any of these **9** mind-blowing tunes from [**Tokyo Machine**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/276). That's a command, by the way.

**Tokyo Machine** has been loved on osu! since the day he was introduced — almost literally. Witness this play of [OKAY](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/508998), one of the first maps to enter the Loved category, then poke through the rest of these epic songs:
