Liquid drum & bass duo **Hybrid Minds** slides into our Featured Artist library.

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/310/header.jpg)

Representing the perfectly mended minds of musicians Josh White and Matt Lowe, **Hybrid Minds** has been innovating the drum & bass scene since its inception in 2011. Tracks like [Kismet](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/340744), [Supernova](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/1823175), and [Drowning In You](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmapsets/1603851) have cemented their places in osu! history, and have earned millions of plays across streaming platforms outside of the game.

**5** buttery smooth tunes can be found on [**Hybrid Minds**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/310), all prepared and ready for your mapping touch.

Want a taste of **Hybrid Minds**? Check out this video showcasing osu!(lazer)'s latest look for osu! and osu!mania in the new "argon" skin, then sift through the rest of the beatmaps and previews:
