Through help from independent electronic label [**Monstercat**](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/255), **MUZZ** enters the limelight as our latest Featured Artist!

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/261/header.jpg)

British producer **MUZZ** (previously known as **Muzzy**) has been a pioneer in the EDM scene for over a decade. *Rocket League*, *Beat Saber*, *Fast & Furious*, and even more video games have adopted **MUZZ**'s fabled sound, with osu! proudly joining this list today.

Take your pick from **12** energetic tracks including classics like *Children of Hell* and *Endgame* — all available from [**MUZZ**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/261)!

See how **MUZZ**'s sound translates to mapping through the video below, then try playing any of the awesome maps below:
