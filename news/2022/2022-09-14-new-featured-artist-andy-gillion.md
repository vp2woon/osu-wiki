Guitarist **Andy Gillion** shines onto our Featured Artist collection!

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/302/header.jpg)

**Andy Gillion** has been a professional guitarist for over a decade. His career launched as the lead guitarist and songwriter for metal band *Mors Principium Est*, later shifting focus towards his solo releases *Neverafter* and *Arcade Metal* — two distinct instrumental albums with one obvious commonality: *the guitar*.

**13** rock/metal masterpieces are pre-timed and ready for you to map. Check out [**Andy Gillion**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/302) for the full list!
