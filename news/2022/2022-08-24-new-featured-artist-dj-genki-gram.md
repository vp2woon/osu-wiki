**DJ Genki** is finally here!

![](https://assets.ppy.sh/artists/295/header.jpg)

If you haven't heard of **DJ Genki**, can you really call yourself an osu! player? Known for being one of the top tier producers of happy hardcore tunes as **DJ Genki** and aggressive rhythm game tracks as **Gram**, this ultra-talented creator has been a household name on osu! for more than half a decade. At long last, he's here to join us as an official Featured Artist!

We've picked up **37** tracks. That's a lot. Find them on [**DJ Genki / Gram**'s Featured Artist listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/beatmaps/artists/295).

You obviously need no introduction to this artist, but we'll provide one anyway. Experience *DJ Genki vs. Camellia - YELL! feat. moimoi* in 3 different modes through this video, then check out the exhaustive catalogue of **DJ Genki / Gram** beatmaps below!
